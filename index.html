A better client for all CrazyGames games, with no ads or annoying UI.


Option 1: Use the Bookmarklet (Recommended)

To easily upgrade your gaming experience, drag the button below to your bookmarks bar.

Then, when you're on a CrazyGames game page, just click the bookmarklet!

Now, head over to the main CrazyGames website, find a game, then click your new bookmarklet! It's that easy!

Alternatively, find a game on CrazyGames. The URL in your address bar will look something like
https://www.crazygames.com/game/your-game-slug.
Copy the your-game-slug part and paste it below.
